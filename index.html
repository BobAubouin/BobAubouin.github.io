About me

I am a PhD student at Gipsa-Lab in the team MODUS (Modelling and Optimal Decision for Uncertain Systems). My thesis is entitled "Data-based anesthesia process modelling for online monitoring and prediction" and is under the supervision of Thao Dang and Mirko Fiacchini. My research interests include Model Predictive Control, the use of Machine Learning for complex systems modelling, and Set Theory for robust control.

I graduated from Ecole Centrale de Lyon and Université Claude-Bernard in Engineering and Control Theory in 2021.
